I currently work as the Chief Eggplant Editor of The Spire, the official student newspaper at the College of the Holy Cross. Our focus at the Eggplant is satire and creative writing. I also like to write poetry. My goal, with all of my articles, is to make you laugh; if not laugh, smile; if not smile, wonder, I don't know why I read this, but I'm glad I did. I'm a changed person now.
